# Zenodo DOI Registration

## How to register (5 minutes)

1. Go to https://zenodo.org — sign in with ORCID or GitHub
2. Click **+ New Upload**
3. Fill in the form using the metadata below
4. Upload a PDF snapshot of the site (or just the About page as a PDF)
5. Click **Publish** — you'll receive a DOI like `10.5281/zenodo.XXXXXXX`
6. Send me the DOI and I'll add it to the site citation format

## Zenodo Metadata

**Title:**
Fī Riyāḍ Tafsīr al-Qurʾān al-Karīm: A Digital Scholarly Edition and Research Platform

**Authors:**
- Kunateh, Amadu (Translator & Digital Editor)

**Description:**
A digital scholarly edition and research platform for *Fī Riyāḍ Tafsīr al-Qurʾān al-Karīm* 
by Shaykh Ibrāhīm Niasse (d. 1975), compiled by Muḥammad ibn Shaykh ʿAbd Allāh 
al-Tijānī al-Ibrāhīmī (10 vols.). The platform presents the complete Arabic text of 
the first cycle of the tafsīr (30 lessons, Suras 1–16), a partial English translation 
(Lessons 1–2), and a critical apparatus of 798 footnotes classified by genre. Research 
tools include a verse concordance (1,529 entries), a scholar and source index, a 
glossary of key terms, and full-text search. Available at niassetafsir.com.
